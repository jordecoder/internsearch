# Suggested company boards to add

Greenhouse board tokens usually come from URLs like:

```text
https://job-boards.greenhouse.io/companyname
```

Use the final part as the board token.

Lever company slugs usually come from URLs like:

```text
https://jobs.lever.co/companyname
```

Use the final part as the company slug.
